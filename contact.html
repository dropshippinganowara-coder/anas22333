Common Questions

How long does delivery take?

We deliver within Chittagong district in 1–2 business days. Outside Chittagong usually takes 3–5 days.

Can I return or exchange a product?

Yes! We accept returns and exchanges within 7 days of delivery. Please contact us first to initiate the process.

Do you offer cash on delivery?

Absolutely. We support cash on delivery (COD) for all orders across Bangladesh.

How do I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, we'll send you a tracking number via WhatsApp or Facebook Messenger.
