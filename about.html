Who We Are

The brand born for Gen-Z fashion

Golden Heart is a young and passionate e-commerce brand based in Anowara, Chittagong. We started just 2 months ago with one clear goal — to bring the best quality t-shirts to the young generation of Bangladesh at affordable prices.

We believe fashion should be bold, authentic, and accessible. Every t-shirt we sell is hand-picked to match the vibe and style that Gen-Z truly loves.

"Our mission is simple — sell the best t-shirts, deliver fast, and make every customer smile. We are Golden Heart and we are here to stay."
